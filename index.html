About Us

Reliable protection, disciplined workforce, proven operations.

Monisha Security Agency (MSA) is a trusted security and manpower service provider established in 2000, delivering professional workforce solutions across Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.

The company is led by Mrs. Usha, Proprietor of MSA, with the strategic leadership of Lion Dr. J.R. Selvakumar, CEO, who drives the organization with a strong focus on discipline, compliance, and service excellence.

With 25+ years of experience, MSA specializes in Security Services, Housekeeping, Manpower Outsourcing, Labour Contracting, and Facility Management for government, industrial, and corporate clients.

Our commitment is to provide reliable, trained, and customer-focused solutions that ensure safety, efficiency, and operational excellence.
